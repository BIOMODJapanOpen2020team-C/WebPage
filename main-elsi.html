ELSI

Our project is trying to create an artificial system to defend ourbody from the pathogen. But if we look at its dark side, thesame technology could be used to offend our body. It may cause new diseases or even pandemics. Namely, the functionality of proposed system (e.g. enhancedimmunity) has conflicting merits and risks. Making artificial immune system can also be understood as anattempt to replace a part of life with an artificial substitute. In this direction, it eventually leads us to have the potential tocreate the life itself. We need to have a thorough discussion on how such manmade or man modified “life” will affect humanity and nature as awhole.
